About

Welcome to the about pages. Far from any definition of "normal" (even for an IT guy), I enjoy being a little bit of an enigma.

I was born in Florida, and grew up in Hernando County, a little less than an hour north of Tampa and St. Petersburg. I showed an early aptitude for technology, spirituality, and music and these three elements still make up the bedrock of this thing called "me." My twenties were a time of struggles, trials, and triumphs and I somehow lived to tell the tale. By far, the greatest event of that period was meeting my wife (and my stepson; more on the kids later!). Now in my thirties--well, we'll get to that...
